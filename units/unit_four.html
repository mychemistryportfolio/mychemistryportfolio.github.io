Course Notes

Course notes are an important aspect of any course. They can be referred to at any time and are especially helpful when studying for tests.
I made highly detailed notes for each organic families and their respective properties such as boiling point and solubility. While making
the notes, I reread the information for each organic family and explained it to an imaginary class to make sure that my understanding of the
organic family was confident. My notes helped me out when I was studying for the quiz. Because my notes were organized in a clear way, I could
easily find the required information when answering practice questions.

Online Simulation

Throughout my academic career, I have recognized that I am a visual learner. I was particularly confused when learning about functional groups.
What confused me the most was how to differentiate between different between groups solely based on their structures. A useful resource I found
was an online website called molview.org. This website allowed me to create different organic compounds and visualize them through a 3-D model.
utilizing this resource, I was able to visually see the differences between different organic families. This let me get a much stronger understanding
of the organic chemistry unit prior to the final quiz of the unit.
